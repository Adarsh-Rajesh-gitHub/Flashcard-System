General Instructions

Adding Flashcards

▼
  • Click the "Add/Edit Flashcards" button on the Flashcard Sets page
  • Fill in the required fields:
    • Subject: The main category or course
    • Topic: Specific subject matter or chapter
    • Front: The question or prompt
    • Back: The answer or explanation
    • Priority: Set importance level (High/Medium/Low)
  • Click "Save Flashcard" to store your new card
  • Your flashcard will be automatically saved and available offline

Editing Flashcards

▼
  • Navigate to the Flashcard Sets page
  • Find the flashcard you want to edit
  • Click the "Edit" button on the flashcard
  • Modify any of the fields as needed
  • Save your changes using the "Update" button
  • To delete a flashcard, use the "Delete" button and confirm your choice

Filtering Flashcards

▼
  • Use the filter options at the top of the Flashcard Sets page
  • Filter by:
    • Subject: View cards from specific courses
    • Priority: Focus on High, Medium, or Low priority cards
    • Topic: Find cards within specific chapters or themes
  • Combine multiple filters for more specific results
  • Clear filters using the "Reset" button

Navigating Flashcards

▼
  • Use the navigation bar to move between different sections:
    • Home: Return to the main menu
    • Flashcard Sets: View and manage all your cards
    • Add/Edit: Create or modify flashcards
    • General: Access these instructions
  • Click on any flashcard to view its full content
  • Use the back button to return to previous pages
  • All changes are automatically saved locally
